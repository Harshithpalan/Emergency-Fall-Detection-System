How detection works: MediaPipe Pose estimates 33 body landmarks per frame, entirely on-device. The hip midpoint's vertical position is tracked over time to build the trail above. A fall is flagged heuristically when vertical velocity exceeds the sensitivity threshold and the body stays near-horizontal and still afterward — this filters out fast movements like sitting down quickly or bending over. Limitations: single camera angle, lighting, occlusion, and clothing all affect accuracy; this heuristic will miss real falls and trigger false alarms. It is a concept demo, not a clinical tool.
